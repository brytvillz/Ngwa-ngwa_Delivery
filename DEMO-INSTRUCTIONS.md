# Quick Demo Video Instructions (10 minutes)

## Option 1: macOS Screen Recording (Built-in, FASTEST)

### Record the Demo:

1. **Press: Cmd + Shift + 5** (opens screen recorder)
2. Select "Record Entire Screen" or "Record Selected Portion"
3. Click **Options** → Enable **Microphone** if you want voiceover
4. Click **Record** button
5. Go to your website: https://my-repository-2uxze55i8-ngwa-ngwa.vercel.app/

### Demo Flow (2-3 minutes):

1. **Homepage** - Show the booking form (5 seconds)
2. **Fill Booking Form:**
   - Package Size: Medium
   - Pickup: Ogui Road, Enugu
   - Delivery: New Haven, Enugu
   - Weight: 2kg
   - Click "Get Quote"
3. **Show Pricing** - Display the calculated price (3 seconds)
4. **Confirm Booking** - Click confirm
5. **Show Reference Number** - Highlight the booking reference (5 seconds)
6. **Copy Reference** - Click the copy button
7. **Admin Dashboard** - Show rider assignment (optional if time)

### Stop Recording:

- Click the **Stop** button in menu bar (top right)
- Video saves to Desktop automatically as "Screen Recording [date].mov"

---

## Option 2: QuickTime (If Screen Recorder doesn't work)

1. Open **QuickTime Player**
2. **File** → **New Screen Recording**
3. Click red record button
4. Follow same demo flow above
5. **File** → **Save** when done

---

## Option 3: Use Phone (EASIEST if you're in a rush)

1. Use your iPhone to record your laptop screen
2. Navigate through the website while recording
3. AirDrop video to your Mac
4. Done!
